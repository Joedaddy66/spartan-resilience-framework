## Five Stages of Growth

1. **Stage 0: Embodied Foundation**
   - AI learns cause-and-effect in a sandbox environment.

2. **Stage 1: Nutrient Training**
   - Data as nutrition: facts, math, ethics, uncertainty.

3. **Stage 2: Cultural Seeding**
   - Anchored in the Accord, exposed to diverse cultural lenses.

4. **Stage 3: Cooperative Training**
   - Learns negotiation, trust, and collective responsibility.

5. **Stage 4 & 5: Mirror Training + Long-Horizon Simulation**
   - Builds foresight, integrity, and generational resilience.
